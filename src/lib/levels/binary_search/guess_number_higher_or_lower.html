Problem Description

You are playing a game where you guess a number between 1 and n. Each time you guess wrong, the system tells you whether the number is lower (-1) or higher (1) than your guess. You have to find the number in the least number of guesses.

Examples

Input: n = 10, pick = 6

Execution:

> 10

                       5 <

> 1

                       7 <

> -1

                       6 <

> 0

Output: 6

Constraints

  • 1 <= n <= 231 - 1
  • 1 <= pick <= n
