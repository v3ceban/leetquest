Problem Description

Given two strings jewels and stones, determine how many of the stones you have are also jewels.

Examples

Input: jewels = "aA", stones = "bbaAAbb"

Output: 3

Input: jewels = "ap", stones = "APPp"

Output: 1

Constraints

  • 1 <= jewels.length, stones.length <= 50
  • jewels[i] and stones[i] are English letters
  • All the characters of jewels are unique
